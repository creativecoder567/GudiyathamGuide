Gudiyatham Bus Stand Timings – Complete Outstation List (2025)

Gudiyatham Bus Stand is the central transport hub for the town and nearby villages, providing a blend of TNSTC public services and private coach operators that connect commuters to regional and intercity destinations. Updated for 2025, this page consolidates daily, overnight and weekend timetables to help you plan trips to Chennai, Bangalore, Tirupathi, Chidambaram, Vellore, Ambur and Kanchipuram, along with frequent local services to Vaniyambadi, Pernambut and Melalathur. The bus stand offers ticket counters and basic passenger amenities; staff at the enquiry desk can confirm live departures and seat availability. Night and special weekend runs are included where available, but schedules can change on holidays—please confirm with the operator before travelling. Frequently Asked Questions

How can I check live bus timings from Gudiyatham?

Contact the Gudiyatham bus stand enquiry counter or call the operator. TNSTC updates may be available via official portals and private operators post timings on booking platforms or phone.

Are there overnight buses from Gudiyatham to Bangalore?

Yes. Overnight private and interstate services operate to Bangalore; book in advance for overnight sleepers or AC coaches.

Do TNSTC buses accept online booking from Gudiyatham?

Booking options vary by route. Many TNSTC routes support counter booking; check TNSTC portals or popular booking sites for online reservations.

What are the local bus timings to Vaniyambadi?

Local buses typically begin around 05:00 AM and run frequently (every 30–60 minutes) until about 09:30 PM; frequency increases during peak hours.
